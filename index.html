余李新千(Lixinqian Yu), is studying at Zhejiang University of Science and Technology (ZUST), China. From September 2019 to present, he majors in Automation at the School of automation and Electrical Engineering. He is also a member of Intelligent Sensing and Decision Making Group, under the tutelage of Le Zhou, Associate Professor. During this period, he won the National Scholarship once, Provincial Government Scholarship once in Zhejiang and First-class School Scholarships twice in ZUST, and so on. At the same time, he is also the recipient of National First Prize in the National Undergraduate Electronic Design Contest, as well as several other provincial competitions.
